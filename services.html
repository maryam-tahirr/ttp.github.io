Procurement Consultancy

Based in Dubai, Thuraya Turnkey Projects excels in procurement services across the Middle East, boasting over a decade of experience. Committed to realizing clients' visions, we deliver unique, high-quality projects tailored to their needs.

With a dedicated team, we ensure top-level service and handle challenges systematically. Our focus is on enhancing projects for hotel owners and operators while saving time and money. Interior and Fit out

Thuraya provides tailored solutions that align with clients' budgets, design preferences, brands, and timelines. Operating across the GCC with offices in the UAE, Oman, Bahrain, Qatar, KSA, and Pakistan, we are a leading interior fit-out contractor.

We transform spaces to meet clients' objectives and budgets, supported by an in-house maintenance team and stringent quality control standards. With over 10 years of experience, Thuraya is a premier UAE interior designer known for fostering friendly, honest client relationships, resulting in thousands of satisfied customers across the GCC.

Furniture Manufacturing FF&E

At Thuraya Turnkey, we design and manufacture outstanding, durable furniture in the UAE. Our Sharjah facility, with over 150 skilled colleagues, produces top-quality interior and outdoor furniture.

We offer an interactive decision-making process, an upholstery department, and comprehensive assembly and shipping facilities. Our experienced, multi-national team ensures the highest quality standards. Our operations team is expertly trained for office furniture and turnkey commercial installations.

OS & E Supplies

Thuraya Turnkey, founded in 2007, is a top FF&E and OS&E Manufacturer, Brand Supplier & Consultant in the UAE & Middle East. Based in Dubai with regional offices in Oman, Qatar, KSA, Bahrain & Pakistan, we serve clients across the GCC.
